# Evaluation 001

## Prompt

Explain cloud computing to a 10-year-old.

---

## Response A

Cloud computing means storing files on the internet instead of on your own computer. It also lets people use apps and services through the internet.

---

## Response B

Imagine you have a magical backpack that can hold all your toys without getting heavy. No matter where you are, you can open the backpack and use your toys. Cloud computing is similar because your files and programs are stored on powerful computers connected to the internet instead of only on your own device.

---
